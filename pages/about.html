About Zee Fabricsplus

Zee FabricsPlus is a textile technology startup with the aim on making quality fabric sourcing easier and more efficient for fashion designers, fabric users and other creative professionals. Our core mission is to grow local textile and fashion manufacturing by providing support for emerging and independent designers.
We have a wide selection of over 1000 quality fabrics at competitive pricing and no/low minimum order quantity. These include adire, cotton, crepe, chiffon, tulle, scuba, velvet, Duchesse, silk/satin, suitings, linen, organza/organdy, Denim, jersey/knits, sequin, specialty fabrics, etc.
We believe progress comes when we innovate and find simpler ways of doing things. This is why we have reinvented fabric sourcing to get the optimal mix of convenience, affordability, best quality, variety and fast fashion.We stand with you as you choose simplicity while embracing your originality and unique sense of style. Love, ZF+ Team.
